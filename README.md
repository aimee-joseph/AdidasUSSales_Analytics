# AdidasUSSales_Analytics
Complete sales-based analysis of an Adidas US dataset, obtained from Kaggle.

## Problem Statement:

Adidas experienced a major revenue jump from 2020 to 2021 across its US retail operations. This project was done to analyse the Adidas US Sales dataset to understand what led to that growth, identify which products, regions, retailers, and sales channels are the most profitable, and provide actionable intelligence to optimise Adidas's US sales strategy.

## Tools & Technologies Used: 

- **Python** (pandas, matplotlib) — data cleaning & EDA
- **Power BI** — dashboard design & visualisation
- **Dataset** — [Adidas US Sales on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sagarmorework/adidas-us-sales)

## Key Insights Observed
- Their revenue grew at least 4x from 2020 to 2021, with the most significant growth occurring between June and December 2021.
- The western region consistently led all other regions in both total revenue and units sold across both years.
- Online sales grew substantially in 2021, which suggests that this shift in customer purchasing behaviour was followed by the pandemic period.
- West Gear was the top-performing retailer, both by revenue and units sold.
